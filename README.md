# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: [Trenutni Datum]**

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how our Discord bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your data.

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Data
- **Server Information**: Server ID, server name
- **User Information**: User ID (not username or personal info)
- **Giveaway Data**: Message IDs, participation records
- **Configuration Data**: Manager roles, VIP roles, log channels

### 2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect
- Usernames or display names (unless in giveaway prize data)
- Direct messages content (except for claim verification)
- Email addresses
- IP addresses
- Personal information

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:
- Operate and manage giveaways
- Track giveaway participants
- Determine random winners
- Manage VIP and manager role permissions
- Send claim notifications via DM
- Log giveaway activities (if configured)

## 4. Data Storage

### 4.1 Storage Location
Data is stored locally in JSON files on our secure server.

### 4.2 Data Retention
- **Active Giveaways**: Stored until giveaway completion and prize claim
- **Guild Configurations**: Stored while Bot is in server
- **Participant Data**: Deleted after giveaway ends and winner claims

## 5. Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share data with advertisers
- Transfer data outside our systems

We MAY share data only:
- When required by law
- To protect our rights or safety
- With your explicit consent

## 6. Data Security

We implement security measures including:
- Secure file storage
- Regular backups
- Limited access to data files
- Error logging and monitoring

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request what data we have about you
- **Deletion**: Request removal of your data (contact us)
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate data
- **Opt-out**: Remove the Bot from your server

## 8. Third-Party Services

The Bot uses:
- **Discord API**: Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
- **Node.js/Discord.js**: Open-source libraries

We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Bot does not knowingly collect data from users under 13. Discord's ToS requires users to be 13+.

## 10. Data Deletion

To request data deletion:
1. Remove the Bot from your server (auto-deletes most data)
2. Contact us at [Tvoj Discord/Email] for manual deletion

Guild configurations are automatically deleted when Bot leaves server.

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date.
